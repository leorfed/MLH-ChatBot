### Core Philosophy: The Pitch as a Story

- **The Primary Goal**: The #1 goal of a 5-minute pitch is to provide just enough information to get the audience interested in speaking with you further.
    
- **Efficiency**: Every slide must serve a clear purpose; if it doesn't, it should be removed.
    
- **Basic Structure**:
    
    - Intro
        
    - Problem
        
    - Solution
        
    - Market
        
    - Business Model
        
    - Go-To-Market (GTM)
        
    - Competition
        
    - Traction
        
    - Team
        
    - Funding/Ask
        

---

### Slide-by-Slide Breakdown (Example: AirBed&Breakfast)

#### 1. Title & Tagline

- **Tagline**: Use a catchy phrase that conveys the essence of the business.
    
- **Example**: "Book rooms with locals, rather than hotels".
    

#### 2. The Problem

- **Description**: Clearly describe the problem the target audience faces and make them "feel" it.
    
- **Validation**: Use statistics or examples to show the significance of the issue.
    
- **AirBnB Example**:
    
    - Price is a major concern for online travel bookers.
        
    - Hotels leave travelers disconnected from local culture.
        
    - No easy way exists to book a room with a local or become a host.
        

#### 3. The Solution

- **Mechanism**: Explain the product and how it specifically solves the described problem.
    
- **Benefits**: Highlight key features.
    
- **AirBnB Example**: A web platform to save money when traveling, make money when hosting, and share local culture .
    

#### 4. Market Opportunity & Size

- **Targeting**: Define the audience and show market growth potential to attract investors .
    
- **AirBnB Metrics**:
    
    - **Total Available Market (TAM)**: 2 Billion+ trips booked worldwide.
        
        +1
        
    - **Serviceable Available Market (SAM)**: 560 Million budget and online trips.
        
        +1
        
    - **Market Share**: 84 Million trips (projected 15% of available market).
        
        +1
        

#### 5. Product

- **Demonstration**: Show, don't just tell; use simple visuals or a demo to convey how it works .
    
- **AirBnB Workflow**: Search by city, review listings, and book it.
    
    +1
    

#### 6. Business Model

- **Revenue**: Explain how the company makes money and outline pricing.
    
- **AirBnB Model**: Takes a 10% commission on each transaction.
    
    - **Average Fee**: Approximately $25 (based on $80/night for 3 nights).
        
    - **Projected Revenue**: $2.1 Billion by 2011.
        

#### 7. Adoption & Go-To-Market Strategy

- **Reach**: Describe distribution channels and sales approaches.
    
- **AirBnB Strategy**:
    
    - **Events**: Target monthly events like Octoberfest (6M people) or Summerfest (1M).
        
        +2
        
    - **Partnerships**: Integrate with cheap/alternative travel sites like KAYAK with a listing widget.
        
        +1
        
    - **Platform Integration**: A "dual posting" feature for Craigslist.
        

#### 8. Competition & Advantages

- **Differentiation**: Identify key competitors and show how the solution is better.
    
- **Competitive Advantages**:
    
    - **1st to Market**: For transaction-based temporary housing.
        
        +1
        
    - **Host Incentive**: Ability to make money compared to sites like Couchsurfing.
        
        +1
        
    - **Ease of Use**: Search by price/date and book in 3 clicks.
        
        +1
        

#### 9. Team

- **Founders**: Include profiles of the founding team and key advisors.
    
- **AirBnB Team**:
    
    - **Joe Gebbia**: Designer/Entrepreneur (RISD graduate), holds a patent for CritBuns .
        
    - **Brian Chesky**: Industrial design consultant (RISD graduate).
        
    - **Nathan Blecharczyk**: Developer (Harvard CS graduate), previously at Microsoft and OPNET .
        
    - **Michael Seibel (Advisor)**: CEO and co-founder of Justin.tv.
        

#### 10. Financials & The "Ask"

- **Funding**: Clearly state the amount needed and the milestones it will enable.
    
- **AirBnB Ask**: Seeking $500K in an Angel Round for 12 months of financing.
    
    +2
    
- **Target Milestone**: Reach 80,000 transactions.
    
    +1
