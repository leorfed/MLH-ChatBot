
# 3. The Solution

## **UW-Crushes: Where AI Meets Campus Reality**

### **Mechanism:**
- A **hyper-realistic voice chat platform** that lets students interact with AI agents modeled after distinct UWaterloo archetypes.
- **Real-Time Voice**: Powered by **LiveKit**, enabling natural, low-latency conversations (**sub-500ms**). LiveKit supports **10 publishers with 3,000 subscribers** on audio-only rooms with real-time conversation metrics.[^1] No typing, just talking.

[^1]: LiveKit Documentation - Benchmarking and Latency Optimization

### **Key Benefits:**
- **Hyper-Local Context**: Agents know *everything* about UW—from the specific disappointment of a laziz bowl to the migration patterns of Mr. Goose.
- **Safe Practice Ground**: Users can practice social interactions, vent, or banter with agents who "get it" without fear of rejection.
- **Vibe Check**: Agents react dynamically to tone of voice and confidence, providing genuine feedback on social cues.
