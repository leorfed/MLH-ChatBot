
# 5. Product

## **Meet Your New Campus Friends**

### **Demonstration:**
- **The Interface**: A "Dating App" style dashboard (Next.js) where users swipe through agent profiles.
- ** The Interaction**: 
    1.  **Select**: Choose a personality (e.g., "The Dax Man").
    2.  **Connect**: Instant voice connection via LiveKit.
    3.  **Converse**: Speak naturally. The agent responds with emotion, sarcasm, and local slang.

### **Core Features:**
- **Distinct Personalities**:
    - *The "Dax"*: Speaks in Slang, emotes, hasn't seen sunlight.
    - *The "Katerina"*: A Slavic Vampire like Girl who never seen the sun.
    - *The "Wei"*: Chinese Waterloo student who do not know how to have fun.
- **"Spotted at UW" Simulation**: Recreates the thrill of anonymous campus posts in a safe environment.
- **Memory**: Agents remember past conversations, building a long-term "friendship".
