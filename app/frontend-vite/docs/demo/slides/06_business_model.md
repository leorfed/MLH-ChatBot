
# 6. Business Model

## **Sustainable Growth & Monetization**

### **Revenue Streams:**
1.  **Freemium Model**:
    - **Free**: Access to basic agents (e.g., "The First Year") with limited daily talk time (15 mins/day).
    - **Premium ($4.99/mo)**: Unlimited talk time, access to exclusive "Celebrity" agents (e.g., "The Dean"), and priority queueing during peak times.
2.  **Token System**: Purchase "Coffee Credits" to unlock special interactions or gift to agents.
3.  **Partnerships (Future)**: 
    - Collaborate with University Wellness services to offer a "lite" version for mental health support.
    - Sponsored agents from local businesses (e.g., "The Bubble Tea Shop Owner" offering real coupons).

### **Projected Metrics:**
- **Conversion Rate**: Targeting **2-3%** of active users converting to Premium, aligned with industry benchmarks:
    - **Freemium mobile apps**: Median **2.18%** conversion rate[^1]
    - **SaaS freemium models**: Typically **2-5%**, with traditional freemium averaging **3.7%**[^2]
    - **Health/fitness apps**: Average **4.2%** conversion (our category)[^1]
- **ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)**: Estimated **$0.50** (blended across free/paid), conservative compared to Replika's **$14M revenue** with **93-person team** in 2024 (~$310K revenue per employee).[^3]

[^1]: Adapty.io - "How to get paid subscribers for your app: conversion from freemium to premium"
[^2]: FirstPageSage - "SaaS Freemium Conversion Rates: 2026 Report"
[^3]: Latka, Statista - "Replika: My AI Friend app revenue worldwide 2025"
