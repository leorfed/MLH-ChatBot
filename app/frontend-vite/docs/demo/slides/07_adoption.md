
# 7. Adoption & Go-To-Market Strategy

## **Going Viral on Campus**

### **Phase 1: Grassroots (The "Reddit" Phase)**
- **reddit.com/r/uwaterloo**: Post "leaked" conversations with the "Cali-or-Bust" agent. The sarcasm will resonate immediately.
- **Discord Communities**: Direct engagement in CS/Eng class servers (20k+ members aggregate).

### **Phase 2: Viral Loop (The "Share" Phase)**
- **"Share Your Cringe"**: One-click export of funny conversation clips to TikTok/Instagram Reels.
- **Referral Program**: "Invite a friend, get 30 mins of Premium talk time."

### **Phase 3: Events & Partnerships**
- **Hackathons**: Demo at Hack the North to showcase the tech.
- **Club Collabs**: Partner with soc-heavy clubs (e.g., EngSoc) for "Speed Friending with AI" events.
